What Bees Do For Us

Bees help us because they are pollinators! A pollinator is any animal that moves pollen from the male flower to the female flower of a plant so that the plant can produce "fruitful" results. As such, the bee is one of--if not THE biggest pollinator of our ecosystem!

With that being said, if the bee population is not thriving, it will eventually trickle down into us not thriving if nothing is done about it!
